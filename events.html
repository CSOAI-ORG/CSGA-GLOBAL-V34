About the Summit

Executive-Level Hybrid Summit

The AI & Defence Innovation Summit 2026 brings together senior voices from defence, artificial and augmented intelligence, cybersecurity, aerospace, academia, and government.

Designed as a focused hybrid summit, the event convenes decision-makers and subject-matter leaders to examine how trusted AI, advanced defence systems, and emerging governance frameworks are reshaping national and global security.

Location

Toronto, Canada — YMCA Toronto Central

Format

Hybrid (In-person and Virtual options)

Attendance

Registration and approval required. No drop-ins permitted.

Summit Focus Areas

  • AI trust, safety, and governance frameworks
  • AI-enabled defence systems and autonomous technologies
  • Quantum and AI-driven security architectures
  • Emerging defence innovation models
  • Secure communications and quantum-era cybersecurity
  • Governance standards shaping the future of defence and AI

Common Questions

Event FAQ

Everything you need to know about attending the AI & Defence Innovation Summit.

How do I register for the summit?

Click the "Register In-Person" or "Register Virtual" button above to secure your spot through our Luma event page. Registration requires approval, and spaces are limited for in-person attendance.

What's included in my registration?

In-person registration includes full access to all sessions, networking events, meals, and summit materials. Virtual registration includes live streaming access to all keynote and panel sessions, plus digital Q&A participation.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend?

Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event. Within 30 days, you may transfer your registration to another attendee or receive a credit for future CSGA events.

Will sessions be recorded?

Yes, most sessions will be recorded and made available to registered attendees for 90 days after the event. Some closed-door roundtable discussions may not be recorded.

Is there a dress code?

Business professional or business casual attire is recommended. The summit is an executive-level event, and attendees typically wear suits or professional attire.

How can my company become a sponsor?

Review our sponsorship tiers above and contact us at cfo@csga-global.org to discuss custom packages. We offer Bronze, Silver, and custom strategic partnership opportunities.
