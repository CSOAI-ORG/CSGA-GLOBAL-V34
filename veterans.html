Program Overview

Building Cyber Warriors for the Digital Battlefield

The CSGA Veterans Program is specifically designed for military veterans transitioning to civilian careers. We recognize the unique skills veterans bring—discipline, leadership, security clearance experience, and mission-focused mindset—and translate them into cybersecurity excellence.

Our comprehensive 12-week program combines technical training, hands-on labs, mentorship from industry veterans, and direct pathways to employment with leading cybersecurity employers.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Veterans Program.

Who is eligible for the Veterans Program?

The program is open to all U.S. military veterans, including active duty transitioning to civilian life, National Guard members, and Reservists. We also welcome spouses of service members in certain circumstances. No prior cybersecurity experience is required.

Is the program covered by the GI Bill?

Yes! Our program is fully approved for VA education benefits including the GI Bill, VET TEC, and Vocational Rehabilitation. We have a dedicated VA certifying official to help you navigate the benefits process.

What certifications will I earn?

Graduates earn multiple industry-recognized certifications including CompTIA Security+, CompTIA Network+, and the CSGA Cybersecurity Professional Certificate. Exam vouchers are included in the program cost.

What is the job placement rate?

We maintain an 85% job placement rate within 90 days of graduation. Our dedicated career services team works with you throughout the program and continues support for 12 months after graduation.

Can I participate remotely?

Yes! We offer both in-person and fully remote options. The remote program includes live virtual instruction, online labs, and virtual mentorship sessions. All you need is a reliable internet connection and a computer.

How does my security clearance help?

Security clearances are highly valued in cybersecurity, especially for defense and government contracting positions. Many of our employer partners specifically seek cleared candidates and offer premium salaries—often 15-25% higher than uncleared positions.
