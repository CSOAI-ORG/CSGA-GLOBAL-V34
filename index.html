Contact Support

What is CSGA and what do you do?

CSGA (Cyber Security Global Alliance) is a global organization advancing cybersecurity education, workforce development, and responsible AI governance. We offer training programs, certifications, research initiatives, and community building across institutions, enterprises, and nations.

How do I become a member?

You can become a member by visiting our Membership page and selecting the plan that best fits your needs. We offer individual, student, professional, and enterprise membership options with various benefits.

Are the courses certified?

Yes! Our courses are industry-recognized and many are offered in partnership with accredited institutions like BMCC and CUNY. Upon completion, you'll receive certificates that are valued by employers worldwide.

Can I access courses online?

Absolutely! Most of our courses are available online with live virtual sessions, recorded lectures, and hands-on labs. We also offer in-person training at select locations for certain programs.

What is the Veterans Program?

Our Veterans Program provides specialized cybersecurity training and career placement services for military veterans transitioning to civilian careers. We offer scholarships, mentorship, and direct connections to employers seeking veteran talent.

Français

Alliance mondiale de la cybersécurité

La CSGA est une organisation mondiale axée sur sa mission qui fait progresser la formation en cybersécurité, le développement de la main d'œuvre et la gouvernance fiable de l'intelligence artificielle.

Grâce à des partenariats académiques, à des environnements de formation en conditions réelles, à une collaboration internationale et à un modèle d'alliance dirigé par ses membres, la CSGA aide les personnes, les anciens militaires, les établissements et les gouvernements à renforcer la résilience numérique et la confiance dans les technologies partout dans le monde.
