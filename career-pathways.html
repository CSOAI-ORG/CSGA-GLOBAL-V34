Career Progression

Your Journey in Cybersecurity

Explore the different career stages and the skills needed at each level

Level 1

Entry Level

0-2 years experience

  • Security Analyst
  • SOC Analyst
  • Junior Penetration Tester
  • IT Support Specialist

Salary Range: $55K - $75K

Key Cert: CompTIA Security+, CEH

Level 2

Mid Level

2-5 years experience

  • Security Engineer
  • Incident Response Analyst
  • Threat Hunter
  • Security Consultant

Salary Range: $75K - $110K

Key Cert: CISSP, GCIH, OSCP

Level 3

Senior Level

5-10 years experience

  • Senior Security Engineer
  • Security Architect
  • Threat Intelligence Lead
  • Security Operations Manager

Salary Range: $110K - $160K

Key Cert: CISSP, CCSP, GSE

Level 4

Executive Level

10+ years experience

  • CISO (Chief Information Security Officer)
  • VP of Security
  • Director of Cybersecurity
  • Security Strategist

Salary Range: $160K - $300K+

Key Cert: CISSP, CISM, CCISO
