Founded in 2021, CSGA has evolved into one of the world's largest global cyber alliances, uniting leaders in cybersecurity education, workforce development, and responsible AI governance.

Our Mission

Uniting Cybersecurity Leaders Worldwide

We unite cybersecurity leaders, educators, and policymakers to build workforce pathways and governance standards that keep organizations and communities secure.

Our focus spans education, workforce development, cyber resilience, and trusted technology governance—creating a comprehensive approach to global cybersecurity challenges.

Advance cybersecurity education globally
Develop workforce pathways
Promote responsible AI governance
Shape international standards

CSGA operates in strategic alignment with Terranova Aerospace and Defense Group, extending our cybersecurity expertise into critical infrastructure protection and aerospace security.
