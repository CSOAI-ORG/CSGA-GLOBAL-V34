FAQ

CSOAI Questions

Everything you need to know about our AI governance services.

What is CSOAI?

CSOAI (Council for the Safety of Artificial Intelligence) is a dedicated initiative under CSGA focused on responsible AI governance. We provide risk assessment, policy alignment, and sector-specific accreditation services.

Who needs AI governance services?

Any organization deploying AI systems can benefit from our services. This includes enterprises in healthcare, finance, defense, technology, and government sectors seeking to ensure their AI is ethical, compliant, and trustworthy.

How long does the assessment process take?

The assessment timeline varies based on the complexity and scale of your AI systems. A typical assessment takes 4-8 weeks, including initial evaluation, risk analysis, and recommendations delivery.

What regulations do you cover?

We cover major AI regulations including the EU AI Act, NIST AI Risk Management Framework, ISO/IEC 42001, GDPR, and sector-specific requirements for healthcare (HIPAA), finance (SOX), and defense.

How do I get started?

Contact us through the form on our website or email us at contact@csga-global.org. We'll schedule an initial consultation to understand your needs and recommend the right services for your organization.
