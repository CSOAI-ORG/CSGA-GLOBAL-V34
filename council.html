Common Questions

Council FAQ

Learn more about joining and participating in the CSGA Global Council.

How can I join the Global Council?

Council membership is by invitation or application. Submit your application through our contact page with your CV and a statement of interest. Our selection committee reviews applications quarterly.

What are the responsibilities of council members?

Council members participate in quarterly meetings, contribute to working groups, provide strategic guidance, and advocate for CSGA's mission. Time commitment is approximately 5-10 hours per month.

Is there a membership fee for the council?

Council membership is complimentary for qualified individuals. We seek diverse perspectives and do not want financial barriers to prevent participation from outstanding candidates.

What expertise are you looking for?

We seek expertise in cybersecurity, AI/ML, policy and governance, ethics, law, defense, critical infrastructure, healthcare, finance, and related fields. Both technical and non-technical perspectives are valued.

Can organizations nominate representatives?

Yes, organizations can nominate senior representatives. Corporate partners and sponsors receive priority consideration for council positions. Contact us to discuss organizational membership.
