FAQ

Education Questions

Everything you need to know about our educational programs.

Are the courses free?

Many of our courses are free for CSGA members. We also offer scholarships for students, veterans, and professionals from underrepresented communities. Contact us to learn about available funding opportunities.

How long do courses take?

Course duration varies: Short workshops run 2-4 weeks, certification programs span 8-12 weeks, and comprehensive career pathways take 6-12 months. All programs offer flexible scheduling.

Are certifications recognized by employers?

Yes! Our certifications are developed in partnership with accredited institutions like BMCC/CUNY and recognized by major employers worldwide. CSGA credentials demonstrate practical skills that hiring managers value.

Can I learn at my own pace?

Absolutely! Most courses offer self-paced modules alongside live instructor-led sessions. You can learn on your schedule while still having access to expert guidance and community support.

What career support do you offer?

We provide comprehensive career support including resume reviews, interview preparation, job placement assistance, and access to our network of 50+ partner employers actively hiring CSGA graduates.
