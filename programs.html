FAQ

Program Questions

Everything you need to know about our training programs.

Who are these programs for?

Our programs are designed for cybersecurity professionals at all levels—from beginners looking to enter the field to experienced practitioners seeking advanced skills. We also offer specialized tracks for developers, managers, and executives.

How long are the programs?

Program duration varies: Short courses run 2-4 weeks, certification programs span 8-12 weeks, and comprehensive tracks like TSO-CSRT take 6 months. All programs offer flexible scheduling with online and in-person options.

Are the certifications recognized?

Yes! Our certifications are developed in partnership with accredited institutions and recognized by major employers worldwide. CSGA credentials demonstrate practical skills that hiring managers value.

What is the cost?

Costs vary by program. Many courses are free for CSGA members, with discounts available for students and veterans. Contact us for detailed pricing and scholarship opportunities.

Can I take courses online?

Absolutely! Most programs offer online options with live instructor-led sessions and self-paced modules. Some advanced programs include optional in-person components for hands-on lab work.
